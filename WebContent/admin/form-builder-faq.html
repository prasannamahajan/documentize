What are fields

textfield : text

numberfield : number

textareafield : textarea

datefield : date

How to create field ?

[placeholder] : [type],[fieldLabel] ,[configProperty:value]

What is card

Form is divided into subparts , at a time only one part is visible , this part is called as card

Each card contains set of related question

card must end with ===

File must end with "end"

What question corresponds to ?

Question is tag , the value in front of this tag is displayed at upper part

You can use html tags in this

What tip corresponds to ?

Tip is extra information provided to user

Sample File

question : Tell me about your information ?
tip : Provide your personal details
1 : text,Name,allowBlank:true,vtype:alpha
2 : textarea,Address
===
question : Tell me about your birth information ?
tip : Provide only your information
4 : textarea,Birthplace
5 : date,Date of Birth
===
question : Tell me about your Company information ?
tip : Provide only your information
7 : text,Company
8 : date,Date of Joining
end
