Name Change Affidavit

By signing this affidavit you will take new name.

You may not be James Bond, but you may use names or aliases other than your legal name. An Affidavit of Name Change can help you prove you're who you say when you've changed your name, but haven't made it official. If you have been using more than one name, create Affidavit of Name Change to verify your identity.
